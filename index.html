Card Collector Game

You will be given a random Target Score at the start of the game.
Each of these four famous baseball cards have been randomly assigned a value.
Clicking on each card adds a specific amount of points to your Total Score.
You win the game by matching your Total Score to the Target Score.
The value of each card is hidden until you click it.
Each time the game restarts, the values of each card will also be re-randomized.
